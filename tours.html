Irish Tours

While home inspection is my vocation my avocation is touring Ireland, the land of my family. If you are interested in a personalized tour of the Emerald Isle just talk to me. I have made over twenty trips to the land that redefines the color green. I have acted as a tour guide/driver multiple times for small groups. The Irish people are welcoming and the trips are always memorable. Call me for more information. Maybe I am biased, but one of my favorite books is How the Irish Saved Civilization by Thomas Cahill.
