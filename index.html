Why Choose Us?

Expertise

I have personally inspected over 10,000 homes since 1992. I am a certified member of the American Society of Home Inspectors and all of my inspections exceed the standards of practice of ASHI®. I am a full time Home Inspector. After graduating from the University of Illinois with a technical degree I built homes in central Illinois. After relocating to Colorado in 1980 I worked for two manufacturers of building products including the Masonite Corporation. During that time, I worked closely with large and small builders as well as architects, engineers, and code agencies in order to insure the correct usage and application of building materials.

Ethics

Murphy Home Inspections is dedicated to providing you a high quality inspection. We do not offer any compensation to third parties for referrals. We adhere to the strictest of ethics as established by the American Society of Home Inspectors. You will always receive the most objective opinion regarding the condition of the home you are buying. My reports will never be influenced by any real estate agent, that is my pledge to you, my client.
